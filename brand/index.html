Mark and favicon

The ri, cut from the wordmark itself

The mark is rendi with the middle letters elided: the wordmark's r flowing into its i in one continuous stroke, first letter to last, the amber dot landing as the i's tittle. Rules

How the brand behaves

  • The dot is the brand atom: the i's tittle, the mark's terminal, the loading pulse, the 16px favicon. Always amber.
  • One accent. Amber never competes with a second brand color.
  • The wordmark draws itself at most once per surface, then rests. Reduced motion gets the finished mark.
  • Never set the wordmark in a font; it is a drawing, not text.
  • Strokes take the surface's ink (currentColor); only the dot is colored.
